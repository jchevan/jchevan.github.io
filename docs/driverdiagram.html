Aim statement

Adults—beginning in midlife—experience equitable, timely, and coordinated cycles of risk identification, early detection, follow-up, and action because evidence-informed brain health approaches are embedded within existing systems, supported by clearly defined and reliable community–clinical linkages, and actively stewarded by public health to maintain alignment and continuity across partners.
